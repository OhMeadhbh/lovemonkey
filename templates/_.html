LoveMonkey is a simple tool to publicly say "thanks" to friends, family & co-workers. When someone's done something nice or clever or happy-making, enter their name and a comment in the Love Generator and click the Button of Love. We'll then send them a note saying how much you love them.

But hey! Why keep your love a secret? You can post your love for someone on twitter or facebook so your twitter followers can express their love too!
